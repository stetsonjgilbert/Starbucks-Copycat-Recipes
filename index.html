Starbucks Copycat Recipes

Caramel Frappuccino

  • 1 cup of ice cubes
  • 1 double espresso shot
  • ½ cup whole milk (or dairy alternative)
  • 2 tablespoons store-bought caramel sauce
  • Whipped cream
  • Extra caramel sauce.

Directions: In a blender, add your ice cubes and your coffee shot. Add your milk and the caramel sauce. Blend on a high setting following the instructions given in our hints and tips. The more you blend, the smoother the drink will be. We find it takes around 30 seconds Pour the coffee and milk blend into a tall glass. Add a large spoonful of whipped cream and then drizzle with a little extra caramel sauce. Finish with a straw. Serve and enjoy!

Pink Drink

  • ½ cup white grape juice
  • 1 cup hot boiling water
  • 4 packs acai berry tea
  • Agave nectar
  • Fresh strawberries
  • 1 to 2 cups of unsweetened coconut milk

    • Directions: Put the unwrapped tea packets in a 2-cup glass measuring cup, and pour hot, boiling water over them. Allow the tea packets to sit until cool, and then squeeze them out and toss. Add the white grape juice in the tea. Add as much ice as you want in a glass and pour one cup of the tea mixture. Fill the rest of the glass with coconut milk. Slice the fresh strawberries and add them to the drink. Add as much agave nectar as you want to make it perfect for your taste. Serve and Enjoy!

Starbucks Vanilla Latte

  • For the syrup
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1-2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • FOr the Vanilla Latte
  • 1 small cup espresso
  • 1 cup whole milk (or alternative)
  • 3-4 teaspoons vanilla syrup
  • Whipped cream

To make the syrup add all of the syrup ingredients to a small saucepan and place over medium heat. Stir constantly until the sugar has dissolved entirely. Once small bubbles appear and the syrup goes a very light straw color, it is ready. Pour the syrup into a heatproof container. Once cooled, cover and store in the refrigerator. To make the Starbucks Vanilla Latte, add your milk and espresso to a small saucepan and place over medium heat. Once the milk begins to bubble slightly, add your vanilla syrup and stir to incorporate. Using either a blender, a mixer, or the technique described above, foam your latte mixture. Pour the foamy latte into a tall glass or mug and garnish with a couple of tablespoons of whipped cream. Sit back, relax and enjoy!
